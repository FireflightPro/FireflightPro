When Fire Threatens What Matters Most

The Rafael Fire of 2021 changed everything. A text message from my mom showed a towering smoke column rising out of Sycamore Canyon, just a few miles south of her home. Two hundred miles away from being of any help, I felt the unique anxiety that comes from understanding wildfire behavior. I'd been a trained wildland firefighter and knew exactly how fire runs up canyons. I was worried for my 85-year-old mom as she prepared to evacuate with her beloved pets and what belongings she could fit in her small vehicle.

That experience ignited a mission: to combine my firefighting expertise with my background in data analytics and emerging technology to help property owners truly understand and mitigate their specific wildfire risks.

Why Trust FireFlight?

As an NFPA Certified Wildfire Mitigation Specialist and FAA Part 107 Licensed Drone Pilot with real-world experience as a wildland firefighter and a background in emergency services and software development, I bring a rare combination of skills to your property's assessment. This unique qualification set allows me to see vulnerabilities others might miss and develop mitigation strategies that truly work for your specific situation.

Beyond Generic Advice: The FireFlight Difference

The standard wildfire preparedness checklist is a starting point, but it's far from sufficient. Generic recommendations don't account for your property's unique surroundings, vegetation, building materials, or the specific ways wildfire might approach your home. They can't prioritize your limited time and resources where they'll make the greatest impact.

FireFlight's approach is fundamentally different. We deliver:

Precision Risk Assessment

Using advanced drone technology and spatial data analysis, we capture detailed imagery and data points specific to your property and identifying vulnerabilities that other inspections often miss. We analyze how wildfire would behave in your specific location, not just in general.

Personalized Mitigation Planning

Your plan will be based on contemporaneous drone imagery, field notes, and fire science, not generic templates. We create a prioritized action plan tailored to your property's unique features and your personal circumstances, ensuring you focus first on changes that deliver the greatest protection.

Comprehensive Support

Beyond assessment and planning, we provide documentation for insurance purposes, connect you with vetted service providers for implementation, and offer ongoing guidance. Your wildfire resilience journey doesn't end with a report. We're your partner throughout the process.
